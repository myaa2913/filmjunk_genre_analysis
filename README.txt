Film Junk Genre Analysis

The three hosts of the Film Junk Podcast (www.filmjunk.com/podcast) review and rate new releases every week. How does the genre(s) of a film affect the rating of each host? 

The genre classifications for each film come from IMDB, accessed via the OMDb API. The ratings are converted from star ratings into a proportion. Ex. 4 out of 5 stars is 0.8; 3 out of 4 stars is 0.75, etc.
